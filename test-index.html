Feng Shui & Geomancy

Geomancy is the reading of energies on Earth and how they affect us.
Geo = Earth Mancy = reading/divination.

Ancient cultures had their own systems of geomancy, some quite sophisticated. [eg. Aboriginal, African, American Indian, Celtic, Chinese, Egyptian, Indian, Nordic, Mayan etc]

Chineses Taoism provides probably the most comprehensive explanation of energy exchanges and Feng Shui specifically relates to assessing living/working environments.

On this site I explain the basic theory and practical design solutions applicable today.
